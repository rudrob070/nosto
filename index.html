What is an endowed scholarship?

An endowed Financial Aid scholarship is invested by Dartmouth and grows in perpetuity

The annual “draw" on its assets allows donors to provide Financial Aid scholarships forever!

How can you contribute to endowed scholarships?

Many are created by an affinity group like a sports team, friendship group, or a reunion class

An endowed scholarship gift starts at $100,000 and can be added to over time

At $1 million plus, a scholarship could finance the cost of one student’s aid in perpetuity
